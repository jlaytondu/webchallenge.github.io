Summary:

The purpose of this project was to begin analyzing the arrest data by Judicial Distict. The analysis began with the 20th Judicial District for review of any trends within types and frequency of arrest charges. The dataset is from the Colorado Information Marketplace within their Public Safety Category and represents data on arrests reported by law enforcement agencies through the Colorado Uniform Crime Reporting Program. One area of interest was to see how arrests were in 2008 and subsequent years during economically challenged times for possible trends with the current economic climate. After assembling the dataset, I used Matplotlib and Seaborn to plot the total count of arrests from 2001 to 2016 for Colorado. The dataset was further edited to review this information among the law enforcement agencies in 20th Judicial District. And then a further analysis of two specific law enforcement agencies that comprise the higher number of arrests within the 20th Judicial District. This site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of this initial analysis, as well as descriptions of any trends.
