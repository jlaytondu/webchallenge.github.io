It should be further noted that the Erie Police Department has jurisidction in an additional Judical District. Their arrest counts were a relatively smaller number so they were still included. The line graph for this jurisdiction had significant variance with Larceny/Theft with the lowest point being in the low 3,000 thousands in 2007 to it's highest just under 6,000 in 2016. The years from 2007 to 2009 resulted in an increase of around 2,000 arrests. The next highest crime type were Assaults with a similar increase from 2007 to 2009 of an estimated 1,000 arrests.
